# against

11.PREP 以...为背景

​	If something **happens** or is **considered against** a particular **background** of events, it is **considered** <u>in relation to those events</u>, because those events are **relevant** to it.

- The Pope has sent a message appealing for unity in his homeland, **against a background** of divisions in the Solidarity movement...

  团结运动中各派搞分裂，**在此情况下**教皇发出号召，呼吁祖国保持团结。

- The profits rise was achieved **against** a backdrop of falling metal prices.

  在金属价格下跌的**背景**下，利润提高了。
