# brute

2.ADJ 粗暴的；野蛮的

​	When you refer to **brute strength** or **force**, you are contrasting it with **gentler methods** or **qualities**.

- He used brute force to take control...

  他靠暴力获得了统治权。

- Boxing is a test of skill and technique, rather than **brute strength**.

  拳击考验的是技能和技术，不是**蛮力**。
