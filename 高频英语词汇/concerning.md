# concerning

1 PREP 关于

​	You use **concerning** to **indicate** what a **question** or piece of information is **about**.

- For more information concerning the club contact I. Coldwell.

  咨询俱乐部详情，请与I.科德维尔联系。

- ...various questions **concerning** pollution and the environment.

  **有关**污染和环境的各种问题。
