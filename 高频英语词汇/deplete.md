# deplete

### 金山词霸网站上 柯林斯高阶英汉双解学习词典 中的词条

##### 1.VERB  (大量)减少，消耗

​	To **deplete** a **stock** or **amount** of **something** means to **reduce it**.

- ...substances that **deplete** the ozone layer...

  大量消耗臭氧层的物质

- They fired in long bursts,which **depleted** their ammunition.

  他们长时间开火，**耗尽了**弹药。
