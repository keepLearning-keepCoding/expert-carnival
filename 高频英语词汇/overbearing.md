# overbearing

### 金山词霸网站上 柯林斯高阶英汉双解学习词典 中的词条

##### 1 ADJ-GRADED  傲慢的；专横的；盛气凌人的；飞扬跋扈的

​	An **overbearing** person tries to **make** <u>other people</u> **do what he or she wants** in an **unpleasant** and **forceful** **way**.

- My husband can be quite **overbearing** with our son.

  我丈夫有时对儿子很**专横**。
