# perimeter

### 金山词霸网站上 柯林斯高阶英汉双解学习词典 中的词条

##### 1.N-COUNT 外缘；边缘

​	The **perimeter** of an **area of land** is the **whole of its outer edge** or **boundary**.

- ...the **perimeter** of the airport...

  机场四周

- Officers dressed in riot gear are surrounding the **perimeter** fence.

  身穿防暴服的警察正在包围**外围的**栅栏。
