### point

##### 7. N-SING (某一）时刻，阶段

 	You use **point** to **refer to a particular time**, or **to a particular stage** in the **development** of something.

- We're all going to die at some **point**...

​     我们都将在某个时间死去。

- At some point, around 70,000 memebers had failed to pay...

  一度曾有约7万名成员没有支付费用。
