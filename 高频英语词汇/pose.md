# pose

2.VERB 提出；陈述

​	If you **pose** a **question**, you **ask it**. If you **pose an issue** that needs considering, you **mention the issue**.

- When I finally posed the question, 'Why?' he merely shrugged.

  当我最后问“为什么”时，他只是耸了耸肩。

- ...the moral issues posed by new technologies.

  新技术带来的道德问题
