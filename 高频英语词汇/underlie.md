# underlie

1.VERB 为...的起因；构成...的基础

​	If **something** <u>underlines</u> a **feeling** or **situation**, it is the **cause** or **basis** of it.

- Try to figure out what feeling **underlies** your anger.

  努力找出你的愤怒之下潜藏的情感。

- ...the energy which seems to **underlie** all human success.

  人类一切成功的背后似乎都不可缺少的干劲。
